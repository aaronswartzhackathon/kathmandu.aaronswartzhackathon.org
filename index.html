Aaron Swartz Memorial Hackathon | Kathmandu

The hackathon will be held on karkhana from November 16 to November 17.

Please Apply here.


What is Aaron Swartz Memorial Hackathon ?

It is a hackathon done in the memory of Aaron Swartz, who had a lot of contributions to the free internet. We focus on solving different problems in this hackathon. More information at www.aaronswartzhackathon.org.

The Kathmandu Event

It will start on November 16 (early morning) and end November 17 (Evening). This is not an competition, and we expect people to come, meet other awesome people, generate/work-on some cool ideas, and most importantly, have fun.
